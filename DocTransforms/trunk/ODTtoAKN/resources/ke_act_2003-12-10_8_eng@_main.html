THE RETIREMENT BENEFITS (AMENDMENT)
ACT, 2003
No. 8of 2003
Date of Assent: 10th December, 2003
Date of Commencement: 19th December, 2003

An Act of Parliament to amend the Retirement Benefits Act, 1997
ENACTED by the Parliament of Kenya, as follows: -

1.

Short title.

This Act may be cited as the Retirement Benefits (Amendment) Act, 2003.

2.

Amendment of section 2 of No3 of 1997

2.

The Retirement Benefits Act, 1997, in this Act referred to as the principal Act, is amended -

  1. (a)

    by deleting the definition of "financial year" and

    substitutingtherefor the following new definition - " financial year" -
    (a) in relation to the Authority, has the meaning assigned to it in section 19;'
    (b) in relation to a scheme, means such accounting period as may be prescribed in the scheme rules;

  2. (b)

    by

    insertingthe following new definitions in their proper alphabetical sequence - " pooled fund" means a fund established by a limited liability company, other than an approved issuer, for purposes of pooling scheme funds for collective investment;
    "statutory fund" has the meaning assigned to it in Cap.487 section 2 of the Insurance Act.

section 54-

54A

Conduct of prosecutions.

(1)

Cap 75.

The Attorney-General may, pursuant to the provisions of the Criminal Procedure Code, appoint public prosecutors for the purposes of cases arising under this Act.
